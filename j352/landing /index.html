Blue Loom

Blue Loom was founded in 2010 by the husband and wife team, Leonardo DiCaprio and Olivia Wolfson-DiCaprio. Tired of living off of acting money, the couple decided to open a restaurant that would add to the Malibu nightlife scene. Blue Loom offers a diverse menu crafted by world class chefs!
