I'm Olivia Wolfson and I'm a Multi-Platform Journalist

I am a Journalism and Government and Politics Major at the University of Maryland. Throughout my time here, I have gained extensive experience in writing articles, producing radio stories, editing articles, and filming news packages. I am currently the Copy Chief for Her Campus Maryland and have freelanced for the Diamondback and WMUC News. I love creating stories that highlight people's successes or bring awareness to certain issues. Everyone deserves to have their stories told and that is what I aim to do!

I grew up in Ocean Township, New Jersey which is only five minutes from Asbury Park and the shore. While at Maryland I have fallen in love with both Baltimore and D.C.
